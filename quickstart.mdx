# Quickstart

Follow these steps to set up your FieldCamp account and create your first job.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up at [fieldcamp.ai](https://fieldcamp.ai) and create your team.

## Step 2: Add Your Team

Navigate to **Settings → Team** and invite your technicians.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/team-setup.png" alt="Team setup page" />
</Frame>

## Step 3: Add Your First Client

Go to **Clients → Add Client** and enter their details.

## Step 4: Create a Job

1. Click **Jobs → New Job**
2. Select the client
3. Add job details (description, service type, location)
4. Assign a technician
5. Set the scheduled date and time

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/create-job.png" alt="Create job form" />
</Frame>

## Step 5: Dispatch and Track

Your technician receives the job on their mobile device. They can update status, add notes, and capture photos as they work.
