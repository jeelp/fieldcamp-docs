# Welcome to FieldCamp

FieldCamp is an AI-powered field service management platform built for small service businesses — plumbers, electricians, HVAC techs, landscapers, and more.

## What is FieldCamp?

FieldCamp helps you manage your entire field service operation from one place:

- **Jobs** — Create, assign, and track jobs from quote to completion
- **Scheduling** — Smart scheduling with drag-and-drop calendar
- **Invoicing** — Generate and send invoices directly from completed jobs
- **Clients** — CRM for your customers with full job history
- **Team Management** — Manage technicians, assign roles, track availability
