## What Are Job Forms?

Job forms are digital checklists and inspection forms that field technicians complete during service visits. They replace paper forms with mobile-friendly digital workflows, ensuring consistent documentation, compliance, and quality control across your team.

<Info>
  **Forms vs. Job Items:** Job items track products and services (billing), while forms track processes and quality control (documentation). Both are essential for professional field service.
</Info>

### Key Benefits

- **Eliminate paper forms** - No more lost clipboards or illegible handwriting
- **Ensure consistency** - Every tech follows the same checklist
- **Capture photos** - Document work with time-stamped images
- **Digital signatures** - Get customer sign-off on mobile
- **Offline capable** - Complete forms without internet, sync later
- **Instant delivery** - Customer receives completed form via email immediately

## Common Use Cases

### Service Checklists

Standardized inspection and service procedures for recurring maintenance work.

**Examples:**
- HVAC seasonal maintenance (20-point inspection)
- Pool service checklist (chemical testing, equipment inspection)
- Pest control treatment log
- Lawn care application record
- Elevator monthly inspection

**Typical Fields:**
- Checklist items (completed/not completed)
- Equipment readings (pressure, temperature, chemical levels)
- Visual inspections (working/needs repair)
- Recommendations for customer
- Technician notes

---

### Safety Checklists

Pre-job safety inspections and hazard assessments to ensure worker safety and compliance.

**Examples:**
- Confined space entry permit
- Electrical safety assessment
- Fall protection checklist
- Personal protective equipment (PPE) verification
- Job site hazard assessment

**Typical Fields:**
- Yes/no safety checks
- Hazard identification
- Mitigation measures taken
- Required equipment verified
- Safety briefing acknowledgment

---

### Equipment Inspection Forms

Detailed equipment condition assessments for preventive maintenance or diagnostics.

**Examples:**

- Backflow preventer test
- Cooling tower inspection

**Typical Fields:**
- Equipment make/model/serial number
- Visual condition assessment
- Performance measurements
- Parts replaced or recommended
- Pass/fail determination
- Photos of equipment tags

---

### Site Inspection Forms

Property condition assessments and project site evaluations.

**Examples:**
- Pre-construction site survey
- Property damage assessment
- Move-in/move-out inspection
- Roof inspection report
- Foundation inspection

**Typical Fields:**
- Location/address verification
- Site condition checklist
- Photo documentation (before/during/after)

- Issues identified
- Recommendations

### Customer Surveys

Post-service feedback collection to measure satisfaction and identify improvement opportunities.

**Examples:**
- Service satisfaction survey

**Typical Fields:**
- Rating scales (1-5 stars)

**Examples:**

## Form Fields

FieldCamp forms support multiple field types for flexible data collection:

### Text Input

Single-line or multi-line text entry for descriptions, notes, and comments.

**Use Cases:**
- Equipment serial numbers
- Work performed descriptions
- Customer comments
- Technician observations
- Part numbers

**Options:**
- Character limits
- Placeholder text
- Default values
- Required vs optional

---

### Number Input

Numeric values for measurements, readings, and quantities.

**Use Cases:**
- Temperature readings
- Pressure measurements
- Chemical levels (pH, chlorine)
- Equipment hours/mileage
- Quantities

**Options:**
- Min/max validation
- Decimal places
- Units (°F, PSI, gallons)
- Calculated fields (sum, average)

---

### Checkbox

Yes/no or true/false selections for binary choices.

**Use Cases:**
- Checklist items (completed or not)
- Safety verifications
- Compliance confirmations
- Feature inspections (working/not working)

**Options:**
- Default checked/unchecked
- Required (must check to proceed)
- Group checkboxes (multiple selections)

---

### Radio Buttons

Single selection from predefined options.

**Use Cases:**
- Condition ratings (Good/Fair/Poor)
- Pass/fail determinations
- Priority levels (Low/Medium/High)
- Service types

**Options:**
- 2-10 options per question
- Horizontal or vertical layout
- Icons/colors per option

---

### Dropdown (Select)

Single selection from a long list of options (space-saving alternative to radio buttons).

**Use Cases:**
- Equipment make/model selection
- Problem category selection
- Technician assignment
- Service location (room, area)

**Options:**
- Searchable (type to filter)
- Custom option entry
- Default selection

---

### Date/Time Picker

Calendar-based date and time selection.

**Use Cases:**
- Installation dates
- Warranty expiration
- Next service due date
- Equipment purchase date
- Inspection timestamp

**Options:**
- Date only, time only, or date+time
- Min/max date restrictions
- Default to today/now
- Auto-populate from visit date

---

### Photo Capture

Camera integration for visual documentation.

**Use Cases:**
- Before/after photos
- Equipment condition photos
- Damage documentation
- Equipment tag/serial number photos
- Site photos

**Options:**
- Required vs optional
- Multiple photos per field
- Annotation tools (arrows, text)
- GPS location tagging
- Auto-timestamp

<Warning>
  **Photo Storage Limits:** Each photo is compressed to reduce storage. Maximum 10 photos per field, 50 photos per form. Large photo uploads require WiFi or strong cellular signal.
</Warning>

---

### Signature Capture

Touch/stylus-based digital signature collection.

**Use Cases:**
- Customer approval (work completed)
- Technician acknowledgment
- Safety briefing sign-off
- Receipt of materials
- Warranty agreement

**Options:**
- Required vs optional
- Signature name field (print name)
- Date/time stamp (automatic)
- Multi-signature forms (customer + tech)

---

### GPS Location

Automatic capture of device location coordinates.

**Use Cases:**
- Site verification (confirm on-site)
- Asset location tracking
- Service area documentation
- Inspection location proof

**Options:**
- Auto-capture on form open
- Manual capture (button press)
- Accuracy requirements (within 50 feet)
- Map display (show location on map)

## Form Assignment

Forms can be assigned to jobs in multiple ways:

### Per Job

Manually assign specific forms to individual jobs.

**When to Use:**
- One-off inspections
- Custom project requirements
- Special customer requests

**How It Works:**
1. Open job detail page
2. Navigate to **Forms** tab
3. Click **Assign Form**
4. Select form template
5. Set completion requirement (required/optional)

---

### Per Visit

Assign forms to specific visits (useful for multi-visit jobs).

**When to Use:**
- Initial site survey (first visit)
- Final inspection (last visit)
- Different forms for different visit types

**How It Works:**
1. Open visit detail
2. Click **Assign Form**
3. Select form template
4. Technician sees form when starting that specific visit

---

### Per Job Type

Automatically assign forms based on job type (e.g., all "HVAC Maintenance" jobs get the HVAC checklist).

**When to Use:**
- Standardized service offerings
- Recurring maintenance
- Compliance requirements

**How It Works:**
1. Navigate to **Settings → Job Types**
2. Edit job type
3. Under **Default Forms**, select form templates
4. All new jobs of this type automatically include those forms

<Info>
  **Automatic Form Assignment:** When creating a job with a job type that has default forms, those forms are automatically added to the job. You can add or remove forms before scheduling.
</Info>

## Form Completion

### Desktop Completion

Forms can be completed on desktop computers (office staff or techs with laptops).

**Best For:**
- Pre-inspection forms
- Office-based quality checks
- Admin tasks

**Limitations:**
- No camera access (must upload photos separately)
- No GPS location capture
- Less convenient for field work

---

### Mobile Completion (Primary Workflow)

Mobile app provides the optimal form completion experience for field technicians.

**Features:**
- Camera integration (take photos directly)
- Touch signature capture
- GPS location auto-capture
- Offline mode (complete without internet)
- Voice-to-text (dictate notes)
- Barcode/QR code scanning

**Workflow:**
1. Tech starts visit (mobile app)
2. App displays assigned forms
3. Tech completes form field-by-field
4. Photos captured with device camera
5. Customer signs on screen
6. Form auto-syncs when online
7. Customer receives copy via email

<Tip>
  **Offline Mode:** Forms completed offline are saved locally and automatically sync when the device regains internet connection. This ensures no data loss in areas with poor cellular coverage.
</Tip>

## Required vs Optional Fields

Form fields can be marked as required or optional, controlling whether technicians can submit incomplete forms.

### Required Fields

Fields that must be completed before form submission.

**When to Use:**
- Critical safety checks
- Compliance documentation
- Customer approval signatures
- Essential measurements

**Behavior:**
- Form cannot be submitted with required fields blank
- Required fields marked with red asterisk (*)
- Mobile app highlights missing required fields
- Error message on attempted submission

---

### Optional Fields

Fields that can be left blank if not applicable.

**When to Use:**
- Additional notes (if needed)
- Optional photos
- Non-critical observations
- Conditional fields (only applies sometimes)

**Behavior:**
- Can submit form with optional fields blank
- No asterisk indicator
- Tech can skip field

---

## Conditional Fields

Show or hide fields based on answers to previous questions (dynamic form logic).

### Example: Equipment Replacement

**Question 1:** Is equipment functioning properly?
- ☐ Yes
- ☐ No

**Question 2 (Conditional):** If "No" selected, show field:
- "What repairs are needed?" (text input)
- "Estimated cost for repair" (number input)

### Example: Safety Hazard Detected

**Question 1:** Are there any safety hazards present?
- ☐ Yes
- ☐ No

**Question 2-5 (Conditional):** If "Yes" selected, show:
- "Describe hazard" (text)
- "Photo of hazard" (camera)
- "Mitigation steps taken" (text)
- "Notify supervisor?" (checkbox)

<Info>
  **Conditional Logic Benefits:** Reduces form clutter by only showing relevant fields. Techs answer fewer questions, improving completion speed while maintaining thoroughness.
</Info>

## Form Versioning

Forms can be updated over time, with version history tracked to ensure compliance and traceability.

### Why Version Forms?

- **Regulatory updates** - Compliance requirements change
- **Process improvements** - Refine checklists based on team feedback
- **Service changes** - Add/remove fields as services evolve

### How Versioning Works

1. **Original Form Created** - Version 1.0
2. **Edit Form** - Modify fields, add questions
3. **Save as New Version** - Version 1.1 created
4. **Historical Forms Preserved** - Completed forms show which version was used

**Version Display:**
- Form header shows version number (v1.2)
- Edit history shows who changed what and when
- Completed forms locked to version used at completion

<Warning>
  **Version Impact:** Editing a form creates a new version. Previously completed forms remain unchanged (they used the old version). Only new form completions use the updated version.
</Warning>

## Form Templates

Form templates are reusable form designs that can be assigned to multiple jobs.

### Creating Templates

1. Navigate to **Settings → Forms**
2. Click **Create Form Template**
3. Build form using form builder (drag-and-drop fields)
4. Save template with descriptive name
5. Template appears in form library for assignment

### Template Library

Central repository of all form templates (pre-built + custom).

**Organization:**
- Industry categories (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, etc.)
- Custom categories (Inspections, Safety, Customer Surveys)
- Search by name or tag
- Filter by industry, category, or creator

**Template Actions:**
- Preview form
- Edit template
- Duplicate template (create variation)
- Archive template (hide from library)
- Delete template (if no forms completed with it)

## Form Permissions

Control who can create, edit, and complete forms based on user roles.

### Admin Permissions

Full form management capabilities:
- ✅ Create form templates
- ✅ Edit form templates
- ✅ Delete form templates
- ✅ Assign forms to jobs
- ✅ Complete forms
- ✅ View all completed forms

### Manager Permissions

Form assignment and oversight:
- ❌ Create form templates (admins only)
- ✅ Edit form templates (if granted)
- ❌ Delete form templates
- ✅ Assign forms to jobs
- ✅ Complete forms
- ✅ View team's completed forms

### Technician Permissions

Field form completion:
- ❌ Create form templates
- ❌ Edit form templates
- ❌ Delete form templates
- ❌ Assign forms to jobs (forms assigned by office)
- ✅ Complete forms (assigned to their visits)
- ✅ View their own completed forms

### Client Permissions (Portal)

Limited view access:
- ❌ All template management
- ❌ Form completion (techs only)
- ✅ View completed forms for their jobs
- ✅ Download completed forms as PDF

<Tip>
  **Permission Customization:** Navigate to **Settings → Roles & Permissions → Forms** to customize permissions per role. Useful for granting managers form creation access or restricting techs from viewing certain sensitive forms.
</Tip>

### For Admins: Setting Up Forms

1. **Review pre-built templates** - Check Settings → Forms for industry templates

## Best Practices

### Form Design

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep forms focused and concise">
    **Why:** Long forms reduce completion rates. Techs skip fields or rush through.
    
    **Recommendation:** Limit forms to 20 fields or less. Break long forms into multiple shorter forms (e.g., "Pre-Service Inspection" + "Post-Service Inspection" instead of one 40-field form).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use conditional logic to reduce clutter">
    **Why:** Showing irrelevant fields confuses techs and wastes time.
    
    **Recommendation:** Hide fields until needed. Example: Only show "Repair Description" if tech selects "Equipment Needs Repair."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mark critical fields as required">
    **Why:** Ensures essential documentation is never missed.
    
    **Recommendation:** Make safety checks, compliance items, and customer signatures required. Leave nice-to-have fields optional.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Provide clear field labels and help text">
    **Why:** Ambiguous labels lead to inconsistent data entry.
    
    **Recommendation:** Use specific labels ("Refrigerant Pressure (PSI)" not just "Pressure") and add help text for complex fields ("Acceptable range: 60-80 PSI").
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Form Workflow

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Test forms with your team before rollout">
    **Why:** Catch usability issues before hundreds of forms are completed.
    
    **Recommendation:** Have 2-3 techs complete a test form in the field. Gather feedback on confusing fields, missing options, or unnecessary questions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review completed forms for quality">
    **Why:** Identify techs who need additional training or forms that need improvement.
    
    **Recommendation:** Managers should spot-check 5-10 completed forms per week. Look for skipped required fields, poor photo quality, or inconsistent answers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Iterate based on feedback">
    **Why:** Forms that don't match real-world workflows frustrate techs.
    
    **Recommendation:** Create a feedback channel (Slack, email) for techs to suggest form improvements. Update forms quarterly based on patterns in feedback.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

### "Form not syncing" (Mobile)

**Cause:** Poor cellular connection or offline mode.

**Solution:**
- Check device internet connection
- Wait for WiFi connection
- Forms saved locally will auto-sync when online
- Manually sync: Settings → Sync Now

---

### "Cannot submit form - missing required fields"

**Cause:** Required fields left blank.

**Solution:**
- Scroll through form to find fields marked with red asterisk (*)
- Fill in all required fields
- Submit again

---

### "Photo upload failed"

**Cause:** File too large or connection interrupted.

**Solution:**
- Ensure strong WiFi or cellular signal
- Retry upload
- If persistent, reduce photo quality in Settings → Camera Quality → Medium

---

### "Form template not appearing in assignment list"

**Cause:** Form template archived or deleted.

**Solution:**
- Navigate to Settings → Forms
- Check "Show Archived" to view hidden templates
- Un-archive template if needed
- Verify user has permission to assign this form

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can customers see forms before the tech completes them?">
    **No.** Forms are only visible to customers after completion. This prevents confusion and ensures final, accurate documentation is shared.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit a form after a technician has submitted it?">
    **Yes, with limitations.** Admins and managers can edit completed forms for up to 24 hours after submission. After 24 hours, forms are locked to maintain audit trail integrity. Edits are logged in form history.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if a tech starts a form but doesn't submit it?">
    **Draft saved locally.** Partially completed forms are saved as drafts on the mobile device. The tech can return to complete the form later. Drafts sync to server when online but are not visible to customers or managers until submitted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I require a photo for certain form fields?">
    **Yes.** When building a form, mark photo fields as "Required." Techs cannot submit the form without capturing a photo. Useful for compliance documentation (equipment tags, before/after work).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do forms work offline?">
    **Yes.** Techs can complete forms without internet connection. Forms are saved locally and auto-sync when the device reconnects. This is essential for basements, remote sites, or areas with poor cellular coverage.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I translate forms into other languages?">
    **Yes (Enterprise only).** Multi-language form templates are available for Enterprise plans. Create form variants in Spanish, French, etc. Techs see forms in their profile language preference.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long are completed forms stored?">
    **Indefinitely.** Completed forms are stored permanently (as long as your account is active). This ensures long-term compliance and historical record-keeping. Forms can be exported to PDF or CSV for external archiving.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
