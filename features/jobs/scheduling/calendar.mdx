## Overview

The FieldCamp calendar is your command center for scheduling and managing visits across your entire team. With multiple views, drag-and-drop rescheduling, real-time conflict detection, and bidirectional sync with Google Calendar and Outlook, you can efficiently schedule jobs, balance workloads, and keep everyone on the same page.

<Info>
**Calendar vs Jobs vs Visits:** The calendar displays **visits** (scheduled appointments), not jobs. A single job can have multiple visits. See [Visit Overview](/features/jobs/visits/overview) for the distinction.
</Info>

---

## Calendar Views

FieldCamp offers four calendar views to match different workflows:

### Day View

The day view shows hour-by-hour schedules for your team with a vertical timeline.

**Best for:**
- Dispatcher managing same-day schedules
- Viewing detailed time slots and conflicts
- Drag-and-drop rescheduling within a single day

**Features:**
- 15-minute time slot resolution
- Multiple tech columns side-by-side
- Color-coded visits by tech or job type
- Overlapping visit detection (visual warning)
- Current time indicator (red line)

**Keyboard shortcuts:**
- `←` / `→` - Previous/Next day
- `T` - Jump to Today
- Click time slot → Create visit
- Click visit block → View details

### Week View

The week view displays Monday–Sunday schedules with each tech in a separate column.

**Best for:**
- Weekly planning and workload balancing
- Identifying open slots for new visits
- Comparing tech schedules at a glance

**Features:**
- 7-day grid (Mon–Sun)
- Condensed time slots (30-minute resolution)
- Visit duration indicated by block height
- Drag visits between techs or days
- Collapse/expand individual tech columns

<Tip>
**Pro tip:** Hold `Shift` while dragging a visit to copy it instead of moving it—useful for recurring visits.

### Month View

The month view provides a high-level overview of scheduled visits across the entire month.

**Best for:**
- Long-term planning (maintenance contracts, recurring visits)
- Identifying busy weeks and scheduling gaps
- Seasonal workload visualization

**Features:**
- Full month grid (Sun–Sat or Mon–Sun)
- Visit count per day (condensed view)
- Color-coded dots by tech or job type
- Click day → Expand to day view

### Visit Time Blocks

Each visit appears as a time block in the calendar grid. The block displays:

- **Client name** (or job description if no client)
- **Start time** and duration (e.g., "10:00 AM • 2h")
- **Address** (truncated)
- **Visit status icon** (Scheduled 📅, In Transit 🚗, In Progress 🔧, Completed ✅)
- **Priority badge** (if urgent)
- **Color bar** (left edge, tech/status/type)

**Block sizing:**
- Height = Visit duration (taller blocks = longer visits)
- Width = Full column width (desktop) or stacked (mobile)

<Tip>
Hover over a visit block to see a tooltip with full details (client phone, job #, assigned tech, notes).
</Tip>

---

### Drag-and-Drop Rescheduling

Reschedule visits instantly by dragging them to a new time or assigning them to a different tech.

**How to reschedule:**
1. Click and hold a visit block
2. Drag to new time slot or tech column
3. Release to update

**What updates automatically:**
- Visit start time
- Visit assigned tech (if dragged to different column)
- Customer notification (optional, confirm in modal)
- Tech notification (via mobile app)

**Drag constraints:**
- ✅ Can drag to any available time slot
- ⚠️ Warning if overlapping with another visit (conflict)
- ❌ Blocked if tech is unavailable (time off, out of hours)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/calendar-drag-drop.png" alt="Drag-and-drop rescheduling" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
Dragging a visit does **not** update the job status. If the job is "Draft," it remains "Draft" even after scheduling a visit.
</Warning>

---

### Overlapping Visits (Conflict Detection)

FieldCamp automatically detects scheduling conflicts and displays visual warnings.

**Conflict types:**
- **Double-booking:** Tech has two visits at the same time
- **Insufficient travel time:** Visit scheduled before tech can realistically arrive
- **Overtime:** Visit extends beyond tech's working hours
- **Skill mismatch:** Tech assigned to visit lacks required skills

**Conflict indicators:**
- 🔴 Red border around overlapping visits
- ⚠️ Warning icon on visit block
- Tooltip: "Conflict: Tech has another visit at this time"

**Resolving conflicts:**
1. Click conflicting visit → View conflict details
2. Options:
   - Reassign to different tech (AI Dispatcher recommends alternatives)
   - Reschedule to different time
   - Override conflict (if intentional, e.g., team visit)

<Info>
Conflicts are warnings, not blockers. You can still save overlapping visits if needed (e.g., emergency jobs).
</Info>

## Creating Visits from Calendar

The fastest way to schedule a visit is directly from the calendar.

### Quick Create (Click Time Slot)

1. Click any empty time slot in the calendar
2. **Quick Create modal** appears:
   - Pre-filled: Date, Time, Tech (from column)
   - Select: Job (search by client or job #)
   - Set: Duration (default 1 hour)
   - Optional: Visit notes
3. Click **Schedule Visit**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/calendar-quick-create.png" alt="Quick create visit from calendar" />
</Frame>

<Tip>
**Keyboard shortcut:** Press `N` to open quick create modal on the currently selected date/time.
</Tip>

---

### Full Visit Creation

For more detailed visit setup (forms, equipment, recurring), use the full visit creation form:

1. Click **+ New Visit** button (top-right)
2. Fill in all visit fields:
   - Job selection (required)
   - Date, Start time, Duration
   - Assigned tech(s) (single or team visit)
   - Visit type (Service, Inspection, Installation, etc.)
   - Visit notes (internal and customer-facing)
   - Forms/checklists to complete
   - Equipment required
   - Arrival window (e.g., "10 AM – 12 PM")
3. Optional: Send customer confirmation (email/SMS)
4. Click **Create Visit**

See [Creating Visits](/features/jobs/visits/creating-visits) for full details.

---

### Drag Job to Calendar

If you have a list of unscheduled jobs, drag them directly onto the calendar to schedule a visit.

**Steps:**
1. Open **Unscheduled Jobs** panel (right sidebar)
2. Drag a job onto a time slot in the calendar
3. Visit is created with job's default duration and assigned tech

## Calendar Integrations

Sync FieldCamp visits with external calendars for seamless scheduling.

### Google Calendar Integration

Bidirectional sync between FieldCamp and Google Calendar.

**Setup:**
1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Calendar**
2. Click **Connect Google Calendar**
3. Authorize FieldCamp to access your calendar
4. Select which Google Calendar to sync to (default: "FieldCamp Visits")
5. Choose sync direction:
   - **FieldCamp → Google** (one-way: FieldCamp visits appear in Google Calendar)
   - **Bidirectional** (recommended: changes in either calendar update the other)
6. Click **Save**

**What syncs:**
- ✅ Visit date, time, duration
- ✅ Client name, address
- ✅ Job description
- ✅ Assigned tech
- ✅ Visit status (Scheduled, In Progress, Completed)
- ❌ Visit forms, photos, logs (FieldCamp-only data)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/screenshots/calendar-google-sync.png" alt="Google Calendar integration settings" />
</Frame>

**Conflict resolution:**
- If visit edited in FieldCamp → Google Calendar updated
- If visit edited in Google Calendar → FieldCamp updated (with 5-minute delay)
- If both changed simultaneously → FieldCamp change takes precedence

<Warning>
Deleting a visit in Google Calendar does **not** delete it in FieldCamp. It only removes the sync link. To delete a visit, do so in FieldCamp.
</Warning>

---

### Outlook / Microsoft 365 Integration

Similar setup to Google Calendar:

1. **Settings → Integrations → Calendar**
2. Click **Connect Outlook**
3. Sign in with Microsoft 365 account
4. Select calendar (default: "FieldCamp")
5. Choose sync direction (one-way or bidirectional)
6. Click **Save**

**Sync behavior identical to Google Calendar.**

---

### iCal Export

Export your FieldCamp schedule as an iCal (.ics) file for import into other calendar apps.

**To export:**
1. Open calendar view
2. Click **Export** (download icon)
3. Select date range (This Week, This Month, Custom)
4. Select techs to include (or "All Techs")
5. Click **Download iCal**
6. Import `.ics` file into Apple Calendar, Outlook, Thunderbird, etc.

<Info>
iCal export is one-way and static (no live sync). Changes in FieldCamp do not update the exported file.
</Info>

---

### Webcal Subscription

Subscribe to a live calendar feed (webcal:// URL) that updates automatically.

**To generate webcal URL:**
1. **Settings → Integrations → Calendar → Webcal Subscription**
2. Select techs to include (or "All Techs")
3. Copy **Webcal URL** (e.g., `webcal://fieldcamp.ai/calendar/feed?key=abc123`)
4. Paste into calendar app:
   - **Apple Calendar:** File → New Calendar Subscription
   - **Google Calendar:** Add calendar → From URL
   - **Outlook:** Add calendar → Subscribe from web

**Webcal behavior:**
- Calendar app checks for updates every 1–24 hours (app-dependent)
- New visits appear automatically
- Cancelled visits disappear
- Read-only (cannot edit in external calendar)

<Tip>
Use Webcal if you want a live feed but don't need bidirectional sync.
</Tip>

## Team Calendar vs Individual Calendar

FieldCamp offers two calendar modes:

### Team Calendar (Dispatch View)

Shows all techs side-by-side for centralized scheduling.

**Best for:**
- Dispatchers managing multiple techs
- Office staff scheduling visits
- Balancing workload across team

**Features:**
- All techs visible in columns
- Drag visits between techs
- Unscheduled jobs sidebar
- AI Dispatcher recommendations

**To open Team Calendar:**
- Click **Calendar** in main navigation
- Default view for Admin and Dispatcher roles

---

### Individual Tech Calendar (My Schedule)

Shows only visits assigned to the logged-in tech.

**Best for:**
- Field techs viewing their own schedule
- Mobile app (primary view)
- Focused, distraction-free schedule

**Features:**
- Single-column calendar (your visits only)
- GPS-based "Start Visit" button
- Time tracking integration
- Visit completion checklist

**To open My Schedule:**
- Click **My Schedule** in main navigation
- Or click your profile icon → **My Calendar**

<Info>
Mobile app always shows **My Schedule** (individual tech view). Desktop allows switching between Team and Individual views.
</Info>

## Calendar Permissions

Control who can view and edit the calendar.

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin** | Full access: view all visits, edit, delete, assign |
| **Dispatcher** | View all visits, edit, assign, reschedule |
| **Tech** | View own visits, mark as started/completed |
| **Sales Rep** | View visits for assigned clients only |
| **Read-Only User** | View visits, cannot edit |

**Custom permissions:**
- **Settings → Roles & Permissions → Calendar**
- Toggle: "Can view all visits" (default: techs see only their own)
- Toggle: "Can edit visits" (default: admins, dispatchers)
- Toggle: "Can delete visits" (default: admins only)

## Calendar Printing

Print schedules for techs without mobile devices or for office reference.

**To print:**
1. Set calendar view (day, week, month, agenda)
2. Apply filters (tech, date range, status)
3. Click **Print** icon (printer symbol)
4. **Print Preview** opens:
   - Adjust: Page orientation (portrait/landscape)
   - Adjust: Font size (small, medium, large)
   - Toggle: Show client phone numbers
   - Toggle: Show visit notes
5. Click **Print**

**Print layouts:**
- **Day view:** Hour-by-hour grid (landscape recommended)
- **Week view:** 7-day grid (landscape, fits letter/A4)
- **Agenda view:** List format (portrait)

<Tip>
For best results, print **Week View** in landscape mode. Agenda view works better for mobile-responsive printing.
</Tip>

## Calendar API

Integrate the calendar into custom apps or dashboards.

**Endpoints:**

```bash
# Get visits for a date range
GET /v1/calendar/visits?start_date=2026-02-01&end_date=2026-02-28&tech_id=123

# Create visit
POST /v1/calendar/visits
{
  "job_id": 456,
  "scheduled_date": "2026-02-26",
  "start_time": "10:00",
  "duration_minutes": 120,
  "assigned_tech_id": 123
}

# Update visit (reschedule)
PUT /v1/calendar/visits/789
{
  "scheduled_date": "2026-02-27",
  "start_time": "14:00"
}

# Delete visit
DELETE /v1/calendar/visits/789
```

See [Calendar API Reference](/api-reference/calendar) for full documentation.

## Best Practices

### 1. Use Week View for Daily Planning

**Why:** Week view balances detail and overview, making it easy to spot gaps and reschedule.

**Workflow:**
- Start each morning in Week View
- Review today + next 3 days
- Identify unassigned visits (red border)
- Drag to assign or use AI Dispatcher

---

### 2. Enable Google Calendar Sync for Field Techs

**Why:** Techs can see their schedule in their phone's native calendar app without opening FieldCamp.

**Setup:**
- Each tech connects their personal Google Calendar
- Visits sync automatically
- Techs receive calendar notifications on their phone

---

### 3. Color-Code by Tech for Dispatch, by Status for Operations

**Why:** Different views serve different purposes.

- **Dispatch:** Color by tech → quickly see who is scheduled
- **Operations:** Color by status → track visit progress (In Progress, Completed)

**Switch colors:** View Settings → Color by → Select scheme

---

### 4. Use Saved Views for Common Workflows

**Examples:**
- "Today - All Techs" (default dispatch view)
- "This Week - Urgent Only" (prioritize emergencies)
- "My Schedule - Next 7 Days" (tech personal view)

**Save once, access everywhere** (desktop, mobile, API).

---

### 5. Resolve Conflicts Immediately

**Why:** Double-bookings and travel time conflicts cause delays and customer complaints.

**Workflow:**
- Check for red borders (conflict indicators) after each schedule change
- Click conflicting visit → View AI Dispatcher recommendations
- Reassign or reschedule immediately

---

### 6. Schedule Visits with Arrival Windows for Customer Flexibility

**Why:** Customers prefer "10 AM – 12 PM" over exact times (reduces no-shows).

**Setup:**
- In visit creation modal, toggle **Arrival Window**
- Set range (e.g., 2-hour window)
- Customer receives: "Tech will arrive between 10 AM and 12 PM"

## Troubleshooting

### Calendar Not Loading

**Symptoms:** Blank calendar, spinner icon, "Loading..." message indefinitely.

**Fixes:**
1. Refresh page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache: Settings → Privacy → Clear cache
3. Check internet connection
4. Check FieldCamp status: [status.fieldcamp.ai](https://status.fieldcamp.ai)
5. Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

---

### Visits Not Appearing in Calendar

**Possible causes:**
- **Filters too restrictive:** Clear all filters (click **Reset Filters**)
- **Wrong date range:** Jump to Today (press `T`)
- **Tech filter active:** Expand to "All Techs"
- **Visit not scheduled:** Visit exists but has no scheduled date (check Unscheduled Visits panel)

---

### Drag-and-Drop Not Working

**Possible causes:**
- **Permissions:** Tech role cannot drag visits (only Admins/Dispatchers)
- **Browser compatibility:** Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest version)
- **Visit locked:** Visit marked as "In Progress" or "Completed" (cannot reschedule)

**Fix:**
- Check role permissions: Settings → Roles → Calendar
- Update browser to latest version
- Unlock visit: Change status to "Scheduled" first

---

### Google Calendar Sync Not Working

**Symptoms:** Visits not appearing in Google Calendar, or changes not syncing.

**Fixes:**
1. Check sync status: Settings → Integrations → Calendar → Google Calendar → **Sync Status**
2. If "Disconnected," reconnect: Click **Reconnect Google Calendar**
3. If "Sync Failed," click **Retry Sync**
4. If still failing:
   - Disconnect Google Calendar
   - Revoke FieldCamp access in Google Account: [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
   - Reconnect Google Calendar in FieldCamp

**Sync delay:**
- FieldCamp → Google: Immediate (< 1 minute)
- Google → FieldCamp: Up to 5 minutes
