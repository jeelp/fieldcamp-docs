# Job Detail Page

The job detail page is command central for a single job. Everything you need to manage, track, and complete a job lives here.

<Info>
  **New to jobs?** Read [Jobs Overview](/features/jobs/overview) first to understand the basics.
</Info>

Access the job detail page by:

  <Card title="From Job List" icon="list">

  <Card title="From Dashboard" icon="home">

### Financial Summary

<!-- Screenshot: sidebar-financial-summary.png — Financial summary card -->

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Subtotal">
    Sum of all job items (before tax and discounts)
    
    **Calculation:** Σ (item quantity × item unit price)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Discount">
    Job-level discount (if applied)
    
    **Types:**
    - Percentage (e.g., 10% off)
    - Fixed amount (e.g., $50 off)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Tax">
    Total tax across all taxable items
    
    **Calculation:** Σ (item subtotal × tax rate) for all taxable items
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Total">
    **Final job value**
    
    **Formula:** Subtotal - Discount + Tax
    
    This is the amount the client owes (or has paid).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Paid">
    Amount the client has paid so far (from linked invoices).
    
    Shows **$0.00** if job hasn't been invoiced yet.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Outstanding Balance">
    **Amount still owed**
    
    **Formula:** Total - Paid
    
    **Color coding:**
    - 🟢 Green: Fully paid ($0.00)
    - 🟡 Yellow: Partially paid
    - 🔴 Red: Overdue (invoice past due date)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  **Pro tip:** The financial summary updates in real-time as you add/remove items or apply discounts.
</Tip>

### Applying Discounts

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Job-Level Discount">
    Applied to the entire job (affects all items).
    
    **How to apply:**
    1. Click **Apply Discount** (below items table)
    2. Choose type:
       - **Percentage** (e.g., 10% off entire job)
       - **Fixed amount** (e.g., $100 off)
    3. Enter amount
    4. Save
    
    **Calculation:** Discount applies to subtotal, then tax is calculated on discounted amount.
    
    <!-- Screenshot: apply-discount-modal.png — Discount modal with percentage and fixed options -->
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Item-Level Discount">
    Applied to a single item.
    
    **How to apply:**
    1. Click **Edit** on an item row
    2. Scroll to **Discount** field
    3. Enter percentage or fixed amount
    4. Save
    
    **Calculation:** Discount applies to that item only.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  **Job-level and item-level discounts don't stack.** If you apply both, only the job-level discount is applied.
</Warning>

<Info>
  Learn more: [Pricing & Discounts](/features/pricing/overview)
</Info>

### Rescheduling a Visit

**How to reschedule:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Reschedule">
    In the visit card, click **⋮ More** → **Reschedule**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pick New Date/Time">
    Calendar opens. Select new date and time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Notify Customer">
    Check **Send notification** to alert customer of change.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Reschedule Visit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Pro tip:** You can also reschedule by dragging the visit on the calendar.
</Tip>

---

### Cancelling a Visit

**How to cancel:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Cancel Visit">
    In the visit card, click **⋮ More** → **Cancel Visit**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Reason">
    Select cancellation reason:
    - Customer cancelled
    - Weather delay
    - Tech unavailable
    - Parts not available
    - Other (enter reason)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Notify Customer">
    Check **Send notification** to alert customer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Cancel Visit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Cancelling a visit **does not delete it**. It marks it as cancelled. You can view cancelled visits by enabling "Show Cancelled" filter.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### I can't edit certain fields

**Solution:** Check your role permissions. Technicians can edit their assigned jobs but may not be able to change pricing or delete jobs.

---

### Photos aren't showing

**Solution:** Photos may still be uploading. Refresh the page. If photos still don't appear, they may have failed to upload from the mobile app — check the mobile app's sync status.

---

### Visit status isn't updating

**Solution:** Status updates happen when techs use the mobile app. If a tech manually marks a visit complete, the status updates. If they don't use the app, you'll need to manually update the status from the desktop.

<Info>
  Learn more: [Visit Status & Tracking](/features/jobs/visits/visit-status)
</Info>

---

### Financial summary doesn't match invoice

**Solution:** If you edited the invoice after creating it, the job and invoice are now out of sync. Changes to job items don't retroactively update invoices (and vice versa).

**Fix:** Edit the invoice directly, or re-generate it from the job.

---

### I can't delete the job

**Solution:** Jobs with linked invoices, payments, or completed visits cannot be deleted. You can **archive** them instead (hides from lists but keeps data).

## Best Practices

### ✅ DO

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Review the Activity tab for disputes">
    If a client disputes a charge or claims work wasn't done, the Activity tab is proof — it logs every change with timestamps.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use before/after photos">
    Photos are powerful for customer satisfaction and future troubleshooting. Encourage techs to take them on every visit.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Add internal notes for historical context">
    If a client calls back with the same issue, internal notes help you understand what happened last time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use custom fields for tracking unique data">
    If you track permit numbers, warranty expirations, or job sources, use custom fields instead of cramming it into the description.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

### ❌ DON'T

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Don't put sensitive info in customer-facing notes">
    Customer-facing notes are visible in the client portal and on invoices. Keep access codes and internal details in internal notes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Don't manually duplicate data across tabs">
    If you add a note in the Details tab, don't also add it as a file or in activity — it's already logged automatically.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Don't change job items after invoicing">
    If the invoice is already sent, changes to job items won't update the invoice. Edit the invoice directly instead.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
