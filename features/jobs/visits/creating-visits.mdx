## Overview

Creating a visit is the process of assigning a technician, date, and time to perform work for a job. FieldCamp offers multiple ways to schedule visits depending on your workflow:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="From Calendar" icon="calendar">
    Drag-and-drop scheduling with visual availability
  </Card>
  <Card title="From Job Page" icon="briefcase">
    Schedule directly from job detail page
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Dispatcher" icon="sparkles">
    Get AI-recommended tech and time slot
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
**Permissions required:** To create visits, you must have the **Dispatcher**, **Manager**, or **Admin** role. Technicians cannot create visits from the mobile app.
</Note>

## Method 1: Schedule Visit from Calendar

The calendar is the primary tool for dispatchers to schedule and visualize visits across all technicians.

### Step-by-Step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Calendar">
    Navigate to **Calendar** from the main navigation menu.
    
    Choose your preferred view:
    - **Day** - See all techs' schedules for one day
    - **Week** - See 7 days at a glance (default for scheduling)
    - **Month** - High-level view of all visits
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Empty Time Slot">
    Click on an empty time slot in the calendar grid.
    
    - **Click specific tech's row** to pre-select that tech
    - **Click time** to set start time
    - The "Create Visit" modal will open
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Job">
    Choose which job this visit is for:
    
    - **Search by job #:** Enter job number (e.g., "J-1234")
    - **Search by client name:** Start typing client name
    - **Browse recent jobs:** See recently created jobs
    - **Create new job:** If no job exists, create one first
    
    <Tip>
    **Pro tip:** Jobs must be in "Draft" or "Scheduled" status to add visits. Completed or invoiced jobs cannot have new visits added.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Date & Time">
    Configure when the visit will occur:
    
    **Start date:** Defaults to the clicked date (can change)
    
    **Start time:** Defaults to the clicked time (can change)
    
    **Duration:** Estimate how long the visit will take
    - Pre-set options: 30 min, 1 hour, 2 hours, 4 hours, 8 hours
    - Custom: Enter specific duration (e.g., 3.5 hours)
    - Smart default: If job has template, duration auto-fills
    
    **End time:** Automatically calculated (start time + duration)
    
    <Warning>
    **Avoid scheduling conflicts:** The system will warn you if the tech already has a visit during this time. You can override, but double-bookings may cause issues.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign Technician(s)">
    Choose who will perform the work:
    
    ### Single Tech Assignment (Most Common)
    - Select one technician from the dropdown
    - Availability indicators:
      - 🟢 **Available** - No conflicts
      - 🟡 **Busy** - Has another visit during this time
      - 🔴 **Off** - Time off or outside working hours
    
    ### Multiple Tech Assignment
    - Click "+ Add Technician" to assign multiple techs
    - Use for: Large jobs, training scenarios, team work
    - All assigned techs see the visit on their mobile app
    
    <Accordion title="Skill Matching (Advanced)">
      If the job requires specific skills (e.g., "HVAC Certified", "Electrical License"), the tech dropdown will:
      - **Prioritize techs** with matching skills (shown first)
      - **Gray out techs** without required skills (can still assign, but warning shown)
      - **Show skill badges** next to tech names
      
      Example: Job requires "Pool CPO Certified" → Only techs with that skill are highlighted.
    </Accordion>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Visit Address">
    Visits default to the job address, but you can override:
    
    **Default:** Use job address (most common)
    - Inherits from job's client address
    - Tech navigates to this address in mobile app
    
    **Custom:** Enter different address
    - Use for: Multi-location jobs, meet at different site
    - Address autocomplete (Google Maps)
    - GPS coordinates automatically saved
    
    <Tip>
    **Why override address?**
    - Equipment stored at warehouse, job site is elsewhere
    - Meet client at office before going to job site
    - Multi-property jobs (pool route, landscaping)
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Visit Notes (Optional)">
    Add internal notes for the technician:
    
    - **Arrival instructions:** "Use back gate, code is 1234"
    - **Special equipment:** "Bring ladder and thermal camera"
    - **Client preferences:** "Customer has dogs, call before entering"
    - **Safety notes:** "Wear PPE, electrical hazard"
    
    <Note>
    These notes are **internal only** (not visible to customer). For customer-facing notes, use job description.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign Forms (Optional)">
    Attach forms/checklists for the tech to complete during the visit:
    
    - **Service checklists** (HVAC maintenance, pool service)
    - **Safety checklists** (site inspection, hazard assessment)
    - **Customer surveys** (post-service feedback)
    
    Forms can also be assigned at the job level (applies to all visits) or visit level (specific to this visit).
    
    Learn more: [Job Forms & Checklists](/features/jobs/forms/overview)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Arrival Window (Optional)">
    Define a customer-facing arrival time range:
    
    **Example:**
    - Start time: 10:00 AM
    - Duration: 2 hours
    - Arrival window: "10:00 AM - 12:00 PM"
    
    **Customer sees:** "Your technician will arrive between 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM"
    
    **Tech sees:** Exact start time (10:00 AM) but knows they have a 2-hour window
    
    <Tip>
    **Best practice:** Use arrival windows for:
    - First visit of the day (traffic variability)
    - Longer jobs (harder to estimate exact arrival)
    - Customers with flexible schedules
    
    Avoid for:
    - Emergency calls (exact time critical)
    - Commercial jobs (specific time windows required)
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customer Confirmation">
    Choose whether to notify the customer:
    
    **Auto-send confirmation** (default): Customer receives email/SMS immediately after scheduling
    
    **Manual confirmation:** You'll send confirmation later (or call customer directly)
    
    **No confirmation:** Internal visit (no customer notification needed)
    
    Confirmation message includes:
    - Technician name and photo
    - Date and arrival time/window
    - Job description
    - Link to track tech (if enabled)
    - Link to reschedule (if self-service enabled)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Visit">
    Click **"Create Visit"** to finalize.
    
    **What happens next:**
    - Visit appears on calendar (color-coded by tech or job type)
    - Assigned tech receives notification (email + mobile push)
    - Customer receives confirmation (if enabled)
    - Visit appears on tech's mobile app schedule
    - Job status updates to "Scheduled" (if previously "Unscheduled")
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Calendar Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up scheduling with keyboard shortcuts:

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `N` | Create new visit (opens modal) |
| `Arrow keys` | Navigate between time slots |
| `Enter` | Open selected time slot |
| `Esc` | Close modal |
| `Ctrl/Cmd + S` | Save visit |
| `Ctrl/Cmd + F` | Search for job or client |

## Method 2: Schedule Visit from Job Page

When viewing a job's detail page, you can schedule visits directly without going to the calendar.

### Step-by-Step

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Job Detail Page">
    Navigate to **Jobs** → Click on a job → **Visits** tab
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Schedule Visit'">
    In the Visits tab, click the **"+ Schedule Visit"** button.
    
    The job is pre-selected (you're already on that job's page).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Scheduling Method">
    You'll see two options:
    
    **Quick Schedule:**
    - Pick a tech, date, time, and duration
    - Fastest method if you already know who and when
    
    **Open Calendar:**
    - Opens calendar view with the job pre-selected
    - Lets you visually see availability before scheduling
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Scheduling (Same as Calendar Method)">
    Follow steps 4-10 from Method 1 above:
    - Set date/time/duration
    - Assign tech
    - Add notes, forms, arrival window
    - Send customer confirmation
  </Step>
</Steps>

### When to Use Job Page Scheduling

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Use Job Page When:" icon="check">
    - You're already reviewing the job details
    - You know exactly which tech and time you want
    - You're scheduling a follow-up visit
    - You don't need to see calendar availability
  </Card>

  <Card title="Use Calendar When:" icon="calendar">
    - You're scheduling multiple jobs at once
    - You need to see tech availability visually
    - You're optimizing routes for the day
    - You're balancing workload across techs
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Method 3: AI Dispatcher Recommendations

FieldCamp's AI Dispatcher analyzes all available technicians and recommends the best match for each job based on skills, location, availability, and workload.

### How AI Dispatcher Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Unscheduled Jobs">
    Go to **Jobs** → Filter by status: **"Unscheduled"**
    
    These are jobs that have been approved but don't have a visit scheduled yet.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'AI Dispatch'">
    On each unscheduled job card, click the **"AI Dispatch"** button (sparkles icon ✨).
    
    The AI will analyze:
    - ✅ Tech skills (does tech have required certifications?)
    - ✅ Tech location (who is closest to job site?)
    - ✅ Tech availability (who has time in their schedule?)
    - ✅ Drive time (minimize travel time)
    - ✅ Workload balance (don't overload one tech)
    - ✅ Historical performance (who completes similar jobs fastest?)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review AI Recommendations">
    The AI presents the **top 3 recommended techs** with confidence scores:
    
    ```
    🥇 Sarah Johnson (Confidence: 95%)
       ✅ Has HVAC Certified skill
       📍 8 miles from job site (15 min drive)
       ⏰ Available: Tomorrow 10am-2pm
       📊 80% on-time completion rate for HVAC jobs
       
    🥈 Mike Chen (Confidence: 78%)
       ✅ Has HVAC Certified skill
       📍 12 miles from job site (22 min drive)
       ⏰ Available: Tomorrow 2pm-5pm
       📊 75% on-time completion rate
       
    🥉 Tom Garcia (Confidence: 62%)
       ⚠️  Missing HVAC Certified skill (can still assign)
       📍 5 miles from job site (10 min drive)
       ⏰ Available: Tomorrow 9am-12pm
       📊 85% on-time completion rate
    ```
    
    <Accordion title="Understanding Confidence Scores">
      **95% confidence:** Excellent match - skill + proximity + availability all optimal
      
      **75-85% confidence:** Good match - most factors align, minor tradeoffs
      
      **<70% confidence:** Acceptable match - some factors don't align (e.g., missing skills, farther away, or lower availability)
      
      **Why recommend lower confidence options?**
      - You might prefer proximity over skills
      - You might need someone available NOW vs waiting for better match
      - You might be training a tech on new skills
    </Accordion>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept or Override Recommendation">
    **Accept AI Recommendation:**
    - Click the recommended tech → Calendar opens with that tech pre-selected
    - You still choose exact date/time (AI doesn't auto-schedule)
    
    **Override AI:**
    - Ignore recommendations and manually select a different tech
    - The AI learns from your choices over time
    
    <Tip>
    **Pro tip:** When you override the AI, you can provide feedback:
    - "Customer requested this specific tech"
    - "Training new tech on this job type"
    - "Balancing workload manually"
    
    This feedback helps the AI improve its recommendations.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### AI Dispatcher Settings

Configure how the AI prioritizes factors:

Navigate to **Settings → AI Dispatcher**

| Setting | Options | Default |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Prioritize skills match** | High, Medium, Low | High |
| **Prioritize proximity** | High, Medium, Low | Medium |
| **Prioritize availability** | High, Medium, Low | High |
| **Prioritize workload balance** | High, Medium, Low | Medium |
| **Allow techs without required skills** | Yes, No | Yes (with warning) |
| **Max drive time** | 15min, 30min, 60min, No limit | No limit |

<Warning>
**Important:** AI Dispatcher is available on **Pro** and **Enterprise** plans. Starter plan users can manually assign techs.
</Warning>

## Team Availability Checking

Before scheduling, always check if the tech is available:

### Availability Indicators

When selecting a tech, you'll see real-time availability:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="🟢 Available" icon="circle-check">
    **Meaning:** Tech has no conflicts during the selected time
    
    **Safe to schedule:** Yes
    
    **What it checks:**
    - No other visits scheduled
    - Within tech's working hours
    - Not on time off (vacation/sick)
    - Not on holiday
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🟡 Busy (Conflict)" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    **Meaning:** Tech already has a visit during this time
    
    **Can you still schedule?** Yes, but warning shown
    
    **Common scenarios:**
    - Overlapping visits (previous job running late)
    - Multiple techs working same job (team work)
    - Emergency override (bump lower priority job)
    
    <Warning>
    **Caution:** Double-booking a tech may cause:
    - Late arrivals (previous job delays)
    - Rushed work (trying to hit both appointments)
    - Customer complaints
    
    Only double-book for intentional team work or emergencies.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🔴 Off (Unavailable)" icon="circle-xmark">
    **Meaning:** Tech is not available during this time
    
    **Reasons:**
    - Outside working hours (e.g., scheduling at 9pm when tech's hours are 8am-5pm)
    - On time off (vacation, sick day, personal day)
    - Holiday (company holiday)
    
    **Can you still schedule?** Yes, but requires manager override
    
    **Use cases:**
    - Emergency after-hours call (tech agrees to work)
    - Adjusting time off (tech cancelled vacation)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="⚪ Tentative" icon="clock">
    **Meaning:** Tech has a visit that isn't confirmed yet
    
    **Examples:**
    - Estimate/quote scheduled (awaiting customer approval)
    - Visit marked as "pending confirmation"
    
    **Safe to schedule?** Proceed with caution - confirm tentative visit won't conflict
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Working Hours Override

Technicians have default working hours (e.g., Mon-Fri 8am-5pm), but you can override:

**Scenario:** Customer needs service at 7am (before tech's normal 8am start)

**Solution:**
1. Schedule visit for 7am (system shows 🔴 Off warning)
2. Click "Override" → Select reason: "Emergency early start"
3. System notifies tech of schedule change
4. Tech can accept/decline override request

Learn more: [Team Availability](/features/jobs/scheduling/team-availability)

## Travel Time Estimation

FieldCamp automatically calculates travel time using Google Maps:

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="System Detects Tech's Location">
    **If tech has previous visit:** Uses previous visit's address as starting point
    
    **If no previous visit:** Uses tech's home address (set in team settings)
    
    **Real-time location:** If tech has mobile app GPS enabled, uses current location
  </Step>

  <Step title="Calculates Drive Time to Job">
    Google Maps API calculates:
    - Distance (miles/km)
    - Drive time (minutes)
    - Traffic conditions (real-time or historical)
    - Optimal route
  </Step>

  <Step title="Displays Travel Estimate">
    You'll see:
    ```
    🚗 15 minutes from previous job (8.2 miles)
    ```
    
    This helps you:
    - Leave buffer time between visits
    - Avoid scheduling visits too close together
    - Optimize routes for the day
  </Step>

  <Step title="Adds Travel Time to Schedule">
    **Option 1 (Recommended):** Add travel time as buffer
    - Previous visit ends at 10:00am
    - Travel time: 15 minutes
    - Next visit starts at 10:15am or later
    
    **Option 2:** Use arrival windows
    - Schedule next visit at 10:00am
    - Set arrival window: 10:00am-10:30am
    - Accounts for traffic variability
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Pro tip:** Enable **Route Optimization** to automatically schedule visits in the most efficient order, minimizing total drive time for the day.

Learn more: [Route Optimization](/features/jobs/scheduling/route-optimization)
</Tip>

## Recurring Visits

For jobs that repeat on a schedule (e.g., weekly pool cleaning, monthly maintenance), you can create recurring visits:

### Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Creating a Visit">
    Follow steps above, but check the **"Recurring"** checkbox
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Recurrence Pattern">
    Choose frequency:
    - **Daily** (rare, but available)
    - **Weekly** (e.g., every Monday)
    - **Bi-weekly** (every 2 weeks)
    - **Monthly** (e.g., 1st of each month, or 2nd Tuesday)
    - **Quarterly** (every 3 months)
    - **Yearly** (annual inspection)
    - **Custom** (every X days/weeks/months)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set End Condition">
    When should recurring visits stop?
    
    - **Never** (ongoing maintenance contract)
    - **After X occurrences** (e.g., 12 visits for annual contract)
    - **Until date** (e.g., end of season, end of year)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Create">
    System generates all visits and shows preview:
    ```
    Creating 24 visits:
    - Every 2 weeks on Monday at 10:00 AM
    - Ends after 24 occurrences (12 months)
    ```
    
    Click **"Create Recurring Visits"** to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing Recurring Visits

After creating recurring visits, you can:
- **Edit one visit:** Change just this occurrence (e.g., reschedule due to holiday)
- **Edit future visits:** Change this and all future occurrences
- **Edit all visits:** Change entire series (e.g., change tech for all visits)
- **Cancel series:** Stop recurring visits early
- **Pause series:** Temporarily pause (e.g., customer vacation)

Learn more: [Recurring Visits](/features/jobs/visits/recurring-visits)

## Conflict Detection

FieldCamp automatically detects scheduling conflicts and warns you:

### Types of Conflicts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Double-Booking (Same Tech, Overlapping Times)" icon="calendar-xmark">
    **Example:**
    - Existing visit: Monday 10am-12pm (Sarah)
    - New visit: Monday 11am-1pm (Sarah)
    
    **Conflict:** Sarah cannot be in two places at once
    
    **Warning:** "⚠️ Sarah is already scheduled for a visit from 10am-12pm. Scheduling this visit will create an overlap."
    
    **Options:**
    - Choose different time
    - Choose different tech
    - Override (for intentional team work)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Outside Working Hours" icon="clock">
    **Example:**
    - Tech's hours: Mon-Fri 8am-5pm
    - New visit: Monday 7pm-9pm
    
    **Conflict:** Outside normal working hours
    
    **Warning:** "⚠️ This visit is outside Sarah's normal working hours (8am-5pm). She may need to approve overtime."
    
    **Options:**
    - Change to within working hours
    - Request overtime approval (system notifies tech)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Time Off Conflict" icon="umbrella-beach">
    **Example:**
    - Tech has PTO: March 15-19
    - New visit: March 17 at 10am
    
    **Conflict:** Tech is on vacation
    
    **Warning:** "⚠️ Sarah is on vacation from March 15-19. Choose a different date or assign a different tech."
    
    **Options:**
    - Reschedule to different date
    - Assign to different tech
    - Cancel/modify time off (if tech agrees)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Skill Mismatch" icon="user-xmark">
    **Example:**
    - Job requires: "HVAC Certified"
    - Tech selected: Tom (does not have this skill)
    
    **Conflict:** Tech doesn't have required certification
    
    **Warning:** "⚠️ Tom does not have the required skill 'HVAC Certified' for this job. Assign anyway?"
    
    **Options:**
    - Choose a certified tech
    - Override (if Tom is training or skill requirement is soft)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
Warnings are advisory - you can still proceed with scheduling. However, conflicts may impact service quality or customer satisfaction.
</Note>

## Bulk Scheduling

Schedule multiple visits at once:

### Use Cases
- Schedule all unscheduled jobs for the week
- Assign all emergency calls to on-call tech
- Schedule follow-up visits for recently completed jobs

### How to Bulk Schedule

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Jobs List">
    Navigate to **Jobs** → Filter by status: "Unscheduled"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Multiple Jobs">
    Check the boxes next to jobs you want to schedule (or click "Select All")
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Bulk Schedule'">
    From the bulk actions menu, choose **"Schedule Visits"**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Scheduling Method">
    **Option 1: Same tech, same day**
    - Assign all selected jobs to one tech
    - System auto-schedules them sequentially (optimizing drive time)
    
    **Option 2: AI Dispatcher**
    - Let AI assign each job to the best tech
    - You review and approve recommendations
    
    **Option 3: Manual one-by-one**
    - Schedule each job individually (default method)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and Confirm">
    Preview the schedule:
    ```
    10 visits will be scheduled:
    - 5 visits assigned to Sarah
    - 3 visits assigned to Mike
    - 2 visits assigned to Tom
    
    Total drive time: 2.5 hours
    Total work time: 18 hours
    ```
    
    Click **"Confirm"** to create all visits at once.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I schedule a visit for this job?">
    **Possible reasons:**
    
    ✅ **Check job status:** Job must be in "Draft" or "Scheduled" status. Completed/invoiced jobs cannot have new visits.
    
    ✅ **Check permissions:** You need Dispatcher, Manager, or Admin role.
    
    ✅ **Check job assignment:** Job must have a client assigned.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why don't I see any available techs?">
    **Possible reasons:**
    
    ✅ **All techs are busy:** Choose a different time or date
    
    ✅ **Job requires skills no tech has:** Override skill requirement or train a tech
    
    ✅ **Tech filter applied:** Clear filters to see all techs
    
    ✅ **Techs are deactivated:** Check team settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customer didn't receive confirmation email/SMS">
    **Troubleshooting:**
    
    ✅ **Check notification settings:** Settings → Notifications → Visit Confirmations (enabled?)
    
    ✅ **Verify customer contact info:** Does customer have email/phone on file?
    
    ✅ **Check spam folder:** Email may be filtered as spam
    
    ✅ **Resend confirmation:** Job detail → Visits tab → Click "⋯" → "Resend Confirmation"
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change a visit that's already scheduled?">
    **Rescheduling:**
    
    - **From calendar:** Drag-and-drop the visit to a new time slot
    - **From job page:** Visits tab → Click visit → "Reschedule"
    
    **What gets updated:**
    - New date/time saved
    - Assigned tech notified of change
    - Customer receives reschedule notification (if enabled)
    
    **What stays the same:**
    - Job items, pricing
    - Visit logs (if any work already completed)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
