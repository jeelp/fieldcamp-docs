# Scheduling

FieldCamp's scheduling system helps you manage your team's calendar and optimize job routing.

## Calendar View

The scheduling calendar shows all jobs across your team. Switch between daily, weekly, and monthly views.

## Drag and Drop

Reschedule jobs by dragging them to a new date or time slot on the calendar.

## Technician Availability

Set working hours and days off for each technician. FieldCamp prevents double-booking and respects availability windows.

## Smart Scheduling

FieldCamp's AI can suggest optimal scheduling based on:
- Technician skills and certifications
- Job location and travel time
- Current workload and availability
- Job priority and deadline
