# Client Management

FieldCamp's client management system is your complete CRM for field service businesses. Track every customer from first contact through lifetime value â€” contacts, jobs, invoices, communication history, and more.

## What is a Client?

A **client** in FieldCamp can be:
- An individual customer (homeowner, property manager)
- A business/company (property management firm, commercial building)
- A lead you're nurturing into a customer

Every client has a complete profile with contact details, addresses, job history, invoices, and communication records.

## Client Types

### Individual Clients
Residential customers (homeowners, renters). Track:
- Personal contact details
- Single or multiple service addresses
- Household preferences

### Company Clients
Commercial/business customers. Track:
- Company name and tax number
- Multiple properties/locations
- Billing department contact
- Website and company address

## Views & Organization

FieldCamp offers multiple ways to view and organize clients:

| View | Best For |
|------|----------|
| **Table View** | Bulk operations, sorting, filtering |
| **Kanban Board** | Visual pipeline, drag-and-drop stage changes |
| **Grouped View** | Organizing by location, tech, status, custom fields |
| **Mobile Cards** | Quick access on phones/tablets |

## Permissions

Client access is controlled by role-based permissions:

- **View All** - See all organization clients
- **View Assigned** - Only clients assigned to you
- **Create** - Add new clients
- **Edit** - Update client information
- **Delete** - Remove clients (with job dependency checks)

Admins and owners have full access. Technicians typically see only assigned clients.
