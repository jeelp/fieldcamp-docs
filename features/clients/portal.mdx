### Step 1: Turn On Portal Access

1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Client Portal**

## Inviting Clients to the Portal

### Sending Portal Invitations

Give clients access by sending an invitation:

1. **Single client:**
   - Open [client detail page](/features/clients/client-detail)
   - Click **‚ãÆ Menu ‚Üí Send Portal Invite**

2. **Bulk invitations:**

   - Select multiple clients (checkboxes)

## Portal Features (Client Perspective)

### Viewing Jobs

Clients see:
- Job number and description
- Scheduled date and time
- Assigned technician (name and photo)
- Job status (Scheduled/In Progress/Completed)
- Service address
- Notes and instructions
- Job total (if configured to show pricing)

**Actions:**
- **View Details** - full job description and line items
- **Reschedule** (if enabled) - request different date
- **Cancel** (if enabled) - cancel appointment
- **Upload Photos** - attach before/after photos

### Viewing & Paying Invoices

Clients see:
- Invoice number and date
- Due date
- Line items (services/products)
- Subtotal, taxes, and total
- Amount paid and balance due
- Payment history
- PDF download link

**Paying an invoice:**
1. Click **Pay Now**
2. Choose payment method:
   - Saved card (if on file)
   - New card
   - Bank account (ACH)
3. Enter payment details
4. Click **Pay $X.XX**
5. Confirmation email sent
6. Invoice marked Paid in FieldCamp

{/* TODO: Screenshot of invoice payment */}

### Booking New Appointments

If enabled, clients can request new service:

1. Click **Book Appointment**
2. Select service type (from your configured options)
3. Choose preferred date and time
4. Add description/notes
5. Upload photos (optional)
6. Submit request

**Result:** Creates a **Draft job** in FieldCamp that you review and confirm.

<Info>
  **Auto-confirm bookings:** Enable "Auto-confirm portal bookings" in settings to automatically schedule client requests without manual review.
</Info>

### Uploading Files

Clients can upload:
- Photos of problem areas
- Documents (contracts, HOA approvals)
- Invoices from other vendors

Files attach to their client profile and are accessible in the Files tab.

### Messaging

If enabled, clients can:
- Send messages to your office
- Reply to your messages
- Attach files to messages

Messages appear in FieldCamp's inbox and can trigger email/SMS notifications to your team.

### Disabling Access for a Client

Revoke portal access:

1. Open client detail page
2. Click **‚ãÆ Menu ‚Üí Disable Portal Access**

If a client forgets their password:

**Option 1: Self-service reset**

**Option 2: Admin reset**

## Security & Privacy

### Authentication
- **Email + Password** - required for all clients
- **Two-Factor Authentication** - optional (recommended for high-value clients)
- **Password requirements** - minimum 8 characters, letters + numbers

### Data Access
- Clients see **only their own data** (jobs, invoices, files)
- No access to other clients' information
- No access to your internal team data or settings

### Permissions
- Admins control which portal features are enabled
- Can disable portal access per client
- Can hide specific jobs or invoices from portal

### Encryption
- All portal traffic uses **HTTPS/TLS encryption**
- Payment data handled by Stripe (PCI-compliant, never stored in FieldCamp)
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt

## Troubleshooting

### Client can't log in

**Check:**
- Is portal access enabled for this client?
- Is email address correct?
- Did they reset their password recently?
- Is the portal enabled globally (Settings ‚Üí Client Portal)?

### Payment fails

**Common causes:**
- Stripe not connected or account suspended
- Card declined (insufficient funds, expired)
- Bank account not verified (ACH)

**Fix:**
- Ask client to try different payment method
- Check Stripe dashboard for error details
- Ensure Stripe account is active and verified

### Files won't upload

**Check:**
- File size under 10 MB
- Supported file type (no .exe, .dmg, etc.)
- Browser allows file uploads (not blocked by extensions)

### Portal page shows "Maintenance"

**Cause:** Portal is temporarily disabled.

**Fix:**
- Re-enable in **Settings ‚Üí Client Portal**
- Check for scheduled maintenance announcements
