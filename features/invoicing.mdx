# Invoicing

Create professional invoices from completed jobs and send them to clients.

## Creating an Invoice

After a job is marked as completed, click **Create Invoice** to generate an invoice with:
- Job details and description
- Line items (labor, materials, parts)
- Tax calculations
- Payment terms

## Sending Invoices

Send invoices via email directly from FieldCamp. Clients receive a professional PDF with your company branding.

## Payment Tracking

Track invoice status:
- **Draft** — Not yet sent
- **Sent** — Delivered to client
- **Viewed** — Client opened the invoice
- **Paid** — Payment received
- **Overdue** — Past due date
