# Requests Overview

Requests (also called Work Requests or Service Requests) are incoming inquiries from customers asking for service. They're the first step in your workflow—before work is scoped, priced, and scheduled as a Job.

## What is a Request?

A request represents a customer's need for service, captured before it becomes a confirmed job. Think of it as a lead or inquiry that needs review, approval, and conversion.

**Key distinction:**
- **Request** = "I need help with my AC" (unconfirmed, unpriced)
- **Job** = "AC repair scheduled for Tuesday, $350" (confirmed, priced, scheduled)

## Request Lifecycle

```
📝 Submitted → 👀 Pending → ✅ Approved → 🔨 Converted to Job
                     ↓
                  ❌ Rejected
```

## How Requests Enter FieldCamp

Requests can be submitted through multiple channels:

### 1. Client Portal (Self-Service) ⭐

Customers submit requests directly via your client portal.

**Perfect for:**
- After-hours requests (customers can submit anytime)
- Recurring customers (they already have portal access)
- Mobile customers (portal is mobile-optimized)

<Tip>
  **Best practice:** Enable client portal requests to capture leads 24/7 without phone calls.
</Tip>

[→ See Client Portal Requests](/features/requests/client-portal-requests)

---

### 2. Phone Call (Manual Entry)

Team member takes a phone call and creates a request manually.

**Workflow:**
1. Customer calls
2. Team member opens FieldCamp
3. Clicks **+ New Request**
4. Enters customer details and description
5. Assigns to tech or sales rep for follow-up

---

### 3. Email Forwarding

Forward customer emails to FieldCamp to auto-create requests.

**Setup:**
- Forward emails to: `requests@yourcompany.fieldcamp.ai`
- FieldCamp parses email and creates request
- Customer email becomes request description

<Note>
  **Email parsing:** FieldCamp extracts customer name, email, phone, and description from email content.
</Note>

---

### 4. Website Contact Form

Embed a FieldCamp request widget on your website.

**Benefits:**
- Captures leads directly from your website
- No third-party forms (data goes straight to FieldCamp)
- Mobile-responsive
- Customizable fields

---

### 5. Online Booking (NEW) 🌟

Customers can book specific services and time slots directly.

**Difference from regular requests:**
- Customer chooses service type (from your catalog)
- Customer picks preferred date/time
- Can include instant pricing (if configured)
- Auto-converts to job (if enabled)

[→ See Online Booking Requests](/features/requests/online-booking)

## Request Components

Every request contains:

### Basic Information

- **Customer details** (name, email, phone, address)
- **Description of work needed** (free text)
- **Priority level** (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
- **Source** (portal, phone, email, website, online booking)

### Additional Fields

- **Preferred date/time** (customer's availability)
- **Photos** (optional - customer can upload photos)
- **Location/address** (service location)
- **Service type** (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, etc.)
- **Custom fields** (configurable per your business)

### Internal Fields

- **Assigned to** (sales rep or tech for follow-up)
- **Internal notes** (team comments, not visible to customer)
- **Tags** (categorize requests)
- **Created date** (auto-stamped)
- **Source** (where it came from)

## Request vs Job: Key Differences

| Aspect | Request | Job |
|--------|---------|-----|
| **Status** | Inquiry, not confirmed | Confirmed work |
| **Pricing** | Not priced yet | Has price/estimate |
| **Scheduling** | No scheduled visit | Visit(s) scheduled |
| **Items** | No items added | Products/services added |
| **Invoicing** | Cannot invoice | Can invoice when done |
| **Customer facing** | Yes (via portal) | Yes (via portal + more) |
| **Typical lifespan** | Hours to days | Days to months |

**Workflow:**
```
Request (inquiry) → Review/Approve → Convert to Job → Schedule Visit → Complete → Invoice
```

## Common Request Workflows

### Workflow 1: Phone Request → Same-Day Job

**Customer calls with urgent issue:**
1. Team member creates request (status: Pending)
2. Assigns to available tech
3. Tech reviews, approves (status: Approved)
4. Clicks "Convert to Job"
5. Schedules visit for today
6. Heads to customer site

**Time:** 5 minutes from call to dispatched tech

---

### Workflow 2: Portal Request → Next-Day Job

**Customer submits request via portal at 8pm:**
1. Request created (status: Pending)
2. Manager reviews next morning (9am)
3. Approves request, assigns to tech
4. Tech converts to job, schedules for tomorrow
5. Customer receives confirmation SMS

**Time:** 13 hours (submitted 8pm → confirmed 9am next day)

---

### Workflow 3: Online Booking → Auto-Converted Job

**Customer books AC tune-up via online booking:**
1. Customer picks "AC Tune-Up" service
2. Selects available time slot (Tuesday 10am)
3. Submits booking
4. **Auto-converts to job** (if enabled)
5. Tech receives notification
6. Customer receives confirmation

**Time:** Instant (no manual review needed)

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Respond Quickly">
    **Goal:** Respond to all requests within 1 hour during business hours.

**Why:** Fast response = higher conversion rate. Customers who wait too long often hire someone else.

**How:**
    - Enable mobile notifications for new requests
    - Set up auto-reply SMS: "Thanks! We'll respond within 1 hour."
    - Assign requests automatically to available techs
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use Request Templates">
    Create templates for common responses:

**Template 1: Approval**
    > "Thanks for your request! We can help with [service]. Our tech will call you within 24 hours to schedule."

**Template 2: Need More Info**
    > "Thanks for reaching out. Can you send a photo of [issue] so we can provide an accurate quote?"

**Template 3: Out of Area**
    > "Thanks for your request. Unfortunately, we don't currently service your area. We recommend [competitor name]."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Track Request Sources">
    Tag requests by source to measure ROI:

- Portal requests (free)
    - Google Ads (paid)
    - Referral (free)
    - Facebook (paid)

**Report:** Cost per request by source → Which channels are most profitable?
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Convert Quickly">
    **Goal:** Convert approved requests to jobs within 24 hours.

**Why:** The longer a request sits, the higher the chance customer finds someone else.

**Workflow:**
    1. Approve request
    2. Immediately convert to job
    3. Schedule visit (even if date is TBD)
    4. Send confirmation to customer
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Emergency Requests

**Scenario:** Customer's water heater is leaking at 10pm.

**Workflow:**
1. Customer submits urgent request via portal
2. On-call tech receives SMS notification
3. Tech approves and converts to emergency job
4. Tech calls customer immediately
5. Schedules visit for same night

---

### Use Case 2: Quote Requests

**Scenario:** Customer wants estimate for new HVAC system.

**Workflow:**
1. Customer submits request via website
2. Sales rep receives notification next morning
3. Sales rep calls customer to schedule site visit
4. After site visit, sales rep converts request to job (estimate type)
5. Sends estimate to customer via email

---

### Use Case 3: Maintenance Contract Renewals

**Scenario:** Annual maintenance contract is expiring.

**Workflow:**
1. FieldCamp auto-creates request 30 days before expiration
2. Sales rep reviews request
3. Calls customer to discuss renewal
4. If customer agrees, converts to job (recurring maintenance)
5. Schedules first visit

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Request not showing in list">
    **Possible causes:**

1. **Filter active** - Clear filters to see all requests
    2. **Permission issue** - You can only see assigned requests (if you're a tech/sales rep)
    3. **Wrong status** - Check if request was rejected or converted

**Fix:** Click "All Requests" view and clear all filters.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't convert request to job">
    **Possible causes:**

1. **Insufficient permissions** - Only admins, managers, and sales reps can convert
    2. **Request already converted** - Check if job already exists
    3. **Missing required fields** - Customer email or phone may be required

**Fix:** Check permissions in Settings → Team → Roles. Or ask admin to convert for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Customer didn't receive confirmation">
    **Possible causes:**

1. **Wrong email/phone** - Check customer contact info
    2. **Email in spam** - Ask customer to check spam folder
    3. **SMS disabled** - Check Settings → Integrations → Twilio

**Fix:** Resend confirmation manually from request detail page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Portal requests not creating">
    **Possible causes:**

1. **Portal disabled** - Check Settings → Client Portal → Requests
    2. **Form misconfigured** - Check required fields
    3. **Browser cache** - Customer needs to clear browser cache

**Fix:** Test request submission yourself to verify portal is working.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
