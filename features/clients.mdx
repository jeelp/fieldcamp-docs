# Clients

Manage your customer database with contact details, job history, and communication records.

## Client Profiles

Each client profile includes:
- Contact information (name, email, phone)
- Service addresses
- Complete job history
- Invoice and payment records
- Notes and tags

## Adding Clients

Add clients manually via **Clients → Add Client**, or they're created automatically when you create a job for a new customer.

## Client History

View a client's complete history — every job, invoice, and interaction in one place. Useful for understanding service patterns and providing personalized service.
