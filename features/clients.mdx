  <Accordion title="Complete Contact Management" icon="user">

    - Custom fields and tags

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead Tracking & Pipeline" icon="trending-up">

    - Conversion analytics
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Complete Activity History" icon="clock">

    - Invoices and payments
    - Email and SMS history

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Client Portal" icon="globe">

## Common Tasks

### Adding Clients
- **[Create a client manually](/features/clients/creating-clients)** - Add individual clients with full contact details
- **[Import from spreadsheet](/features/clients/importing)** - Bulk upload hundreds of clients from CSV/Excel
- **[Sync from accounting software](/features/clients/importing#importing-from-integrations)** - Xero, Wave, QuickBooks integration

### Organizing Clients
- **[Filter and search](/features/clients/client-list#filtering-clients)** - Build complex queries to find specific clients
- **[Create saved views](/features/clients/client-list#saved-views)** - Save frequent filter combinations for quick access
- **[Use kanban board](/features/clients/stages-pipeline#kanban-board-view)** - Visual pipeline with drag-and-drop stage changes
- **[Group by field](/features/clients/client-list#grouped-view)** - Organize clients by city, tech, stage, or custom fields

### Working with Client Data
- **[View client details](/features/clients/client-detail)** - Access complete client profile with all tabs
- **[Track communication history](/features/clients/client-detail#communication-tabs)** - View all emails, SMS, and calls in one place
- **[Manage files and documents](/features/clients/client-detail#files--drive-tab)** - Upload and organize client files
- **[Record payments](/features/clients/client-detail#payments-tab)** - Track payment history and Stripe methods

### Client Self-Service
- **[Enable client portal](/features/clients/portal)** - Turn on self-service features for customers
- **[Send portal invitations](/features/clients/portal#inviting-clients-to-the-portal)** - Give clients access to their account
- **[Customize portal branding](/features/clients/portal#step-3-brand-the-portal)** - Add your logo and colors
- **[Set up online payments](/features/clients/portal#step-4-set-up-online-payments-optional)** - Accept credit cards via Stripe
